Past Projects

Here's a list of personal projects that I've worked on.

  • Facebook Terminal
    • A "geek-friendly" interface for Facebook.
    • Built a javascript app that presents a shell-like interface in which users can access Facebook by issuing commands such as ls, cd, mv, etc.
  • Underwater Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV)
    • I was the president for an underwater ROV team participating in MATE competition.
    • We designed our own waterproof components such as cameras and on-board circuitry.
  • Photo Ordering Website
    • A website for students at my high school to shop prom photos online.
    • Users can tag their friends in photos so it's easier to find them.
    • Payments can be made through the school's meal card system.
  • Chinese Writing App
    • Helped a local university in Taiwan to port their software for teaching Chinese characters from Windows to Android.
    • Designed a new interface in Android while interfacing with existing code written in C++.
  • Volume Control
    • An Android app that automatically changes your phone's volume when you plug in your earphone.
    • This app was created back in the early days of Android (2008) where such feature was missing.
